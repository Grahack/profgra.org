1. **scale**  
   linear : `123456765432` (eighths then sixteenths)
1. **3rds**  
   `1324 ... 721-` then `3127 ... 1-`
1. **triads**  
   `1353 2464 ... 7242 1---`  
   `5313 4272 ...`
1. **chords** (in fact 4 notes)  
   `1357 rest 2468 rest ...` then without rest
1. **approach**  
   thirds with approach from below one the first note  
   `a13- a24- ...`
1. **approach triads**  
   triads with approach from below one the first note  
1. **approach chords**  
   chords with approach from below one the first note  
1. **pivot**  
   `1357` but the 1 is one octave above
