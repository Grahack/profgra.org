## Level 1

* Dm7 G7: F and G (on both chord)
* C Am: G and Am (on both chord)

## Level 2

* Dm: C and Dm
* G7 alt: Db and Eb
* C: D and Em
* A7 alt:  Eb and F

## Level 3

* D7 alt: Ab and Bb
* B7 alt: F and G (haha!)
* C #11 #5: D and E (Am melo)
* Eb7sus: Db and Eb
